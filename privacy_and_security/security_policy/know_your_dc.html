Know Your Datacenter

We host your data in best-in-class datacenters across the globe. When you sign up for Vani, you are given the option to choose the country from which you're signing up from. In order to make it easier for you, that field is selected by default based on your IP address. Based on the country chosen there, the corresponding datacenter is chosen for your account.
