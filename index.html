Nila is where product designers craft the best user experiences on a native Mac app, while bringing the rest of the team into the design process, on the Web.

Hey, product designers. We know you native Mac.

We do too. That's why Nila is natively Mac.

Design on a dedicated space, away from the 100+ browser tabs. Built on Apple's tried and tested frameworks, Nila for Mac feels right at home with the rest of your OS. Just like you, we're big on speed and precision – Nila comes with native font rendering, P3 color profiles, dark UI, offline mode, and everything else you'd expect from native Mac.

All the tools you need to bring your biggest and boldest ideas to life.

Flexible Vectors

Build high-quality screens with powerful vector-based graphics. Nila helps you scale layers with contextual tools that align points, lines, curves and shapes to pixel-perfection.

Auto Layouts

Resize your designs as you add text and create responsive layouts that simply adapt to any changes you make.

Animated Prototypes

Connect screen elements for gestures, actions, and interactive overlays. With Nila, you can make every little transition detail count and handoff the smoothest user experiences with clarity.

Preview designs

All the presentation settings you need so your work is always ready for display. Pre- view any screen or device interaction just as you design them.

Shared Styles

Create Styles for a Text or Layer and apply them anywhere across your designs - all at one go.

Reusable Components

With every Instance connected to the Leader, Components make it a breeze to scale your designs with speed and consistency.
